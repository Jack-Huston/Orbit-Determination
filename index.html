How to use this simulator
  • Drag the orbit window by its header. Use Dock to lock it into the layout.
  • Zoom with mouse wheel/trackpad, or pinch on mobile. Pan by dragging inside the orbit canvas.
  • Each dt seconds of simulation time, visible stations generate measurements (ρ, ρ̇, φ). Pulses animate along links.
  • Change Q/R live for both truth and filter to show estimator robustness and mismatch behavior.
  • Use Δv prograde or continuous accel to perturb the orbit and watch the EKF track.
This is a portfolio demo: the point is to show physics + estimation + visualization + UI polish. If you want even more “DSN Now” feel (antenna cards per station, mission labels, etc.), say so.
